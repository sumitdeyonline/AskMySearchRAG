Transformers is a popular media franchise that originated from a toy line created by Hasbro and a Japanese toy company called Takara Tomy. The franchise revolves around sentient robots, known as Transformers, who can transform into vehicles, animals, and other objects. These Transformers come from the planet Cybertron and are split into two factions, the Autobots and the Decepticons, who are in a constant struggle for control over the universe.

The Transformers franchise includes various forms of media, such as animated television series, comic books, video games, and a series of movies directed by Michael Bay. The concept of the Transformers has captivated audiences due to its unique blend of science fiction, action, and adventure.

One of the key elements of Transformers is the idea of transformation, where the robots can change their forms to adapt to different situations and challenges. This ability to transform adds an element of excitement and unpredictability to the stories, as the robots can easily switch from vehicle mode to robot mode to engage in battles or missions.

Overall, Transformers has become a beloved franchise that has captured the imagination of fans around the world with its thrilling stories, memorable characters, and groundbreaking concept of robots in disguise.
